ENSAK CONNECT

ENSAK Connect is an application developed for the National School of Applied Sciences of Kenitra (ENSA-Kenitra) community with the aim of facilitating students integration into the job market. It provides internship offers for students and graduates, assisting them in finding jobs or internships. Additionally, it is designed for professors to post doctoral offers and support their students in seamlessly integrating into the market.
